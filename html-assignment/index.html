About Me

I am a freelance photographer based in CA with over 4+ years of professional experience capturing moments that tell real and meaningful stories. My journey into photography has been driven by passion, discipline, and a strong desire to visually communicate emotion, identity, and authenticity. I specialize in portraits, lifestyle photography, and creative visual projects, working closely with clients to bring their ideas to life while maintaining a strong personal artistic vision.

My creative vision and imagination led me to build my photography brand studio called Mr.Myeyesonly, a name that represents how I see the world through my own unique perspective. I believe photography is not just about images, but about personal eyesight, emotion, and intention. That philosophy inspired my slogan, “The eyesight studio”, which reflects my commitment to capturing moments exactly as I envision them. Through this brand, I transform imagination into visual stories that feel intimate, bold, and authentic.

“I move forward even when the path feels uncertain, because staying consistent with my vision is how I turn discipline into progress.” — DAVID ZUKO
